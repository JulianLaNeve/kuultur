About Kuultur

Kuultur is a startup based out of Dallas, TX that supports designers from developing nations. We select a few designers each month from a nation, and feature them. Users who pay a flat monthly fee get access to care box every month shipped to them in the mail. These boxes are filled with apparel based on the designs and culture of those nations.
